# 🌿 AyurTrace: Blockchain-Powered Ayurvedic Herb Traceability System

## Smart India Hackathon 2025 Presentation

---

## 🎯 **Problem Statement**

### Current Challenges in Ayurvedic Industry:
- **Lack of Transparency**: No way to verify herb authenticity from farm to pharmacy
- **Quality Concerns**: Difficulty in tracking herb quality and origin
- **Counterfeit Products**: Fake ayurvedic medicines flooding the market  
- **Supply Chain Gaps**: Missing links between farmers, processors, and consumers
- **Trust Deficit**: Consumers cannot verify product authenticity

### **Impact**: 
- Loss of consumer confidence in Ayurvedic medicines
- Farmers not getting fair prices for quality herbs
- Health risks from contaminated/fake products

---

## 💡 **Our Solution: AyurTrace**

### **Vision**: 
*"Creating an immutable, transparent supply chain for Ayurvedic herbs using blockchain technology"*

### **Core Features**:

#### 🔐 **Blockchain Security**
- Built on **Avalanche Fuji Testnet** for fast, low-cost transactions
- Immutable records that cannot be tampered with
- Smart contracts for automated verification

#### 🗺️ **Complete Traceability** 
- Track herbs from **farm to pharmacy**
- GPS coordinates for exact location tracking
- Timestamp every transaction in the supply chain

#### 👨‍🌾 **Farmer Empowerment**
- Direct registration of herb harvests
- Quality certification and grading
- Fair price discovery through transparency

#### 🔍 **Consumer Verification**
- QR code scanning for instant product history
- Complete journey visualization
- Quality assurance certificates

---

## 🛠️ **Technical Architecture**

### **Frontend**: 
- **Next.js 15** with TypeScript for modern, fast UI
- **TailwindCSS** for responsive design
- **RainbowKit** for seamless wallet integration

### **Backend**:
- **Node.js + Express** API server
- **MongoDB** for fast data queries
- **RESTful APIs** for all operations

### **Blockchain**:
- **Avalanche Fuji Testnet** for production-ready scaling
- **Solidity Smart Contracts** for herb registration
- **ethers.js** for blockchain interactions

### **Integration**:
- **MetaMask** wallet integration
- **GPS mapping** for location tracking
- **QR code generation** for easy verification

---

## 🔄 **How It Works**

### **Step 1: Herb Registration** 🌱
- Farmer registers herb harvest with GPS location
- Quality details, quantity, and harvest date recorded
- Optional blockchain verification for premium traceability

### **Step 2: Processing & Distribution** 🏭
- Each stakeholder updates herb status
- Location and processing details tracked
- Quality checks recorded at each stage

### **Step 3: Consumer Verification** 📱
- QR code scan reveals complete journey
- Blockchain verification confirms authenticity
- Consumer gains confidence in product quality
